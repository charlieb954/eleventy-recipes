## info
* About 30 minutes  
* 2 servings

## ingredients
- 150g dried spaghetti
- 4 sausages
- 400g tinned cherry tomatoes
- 1 tsp fennel seeds
- 1 tsp thyme
- 1 tsp chilli flakes
- salt and pepper

## steps
1. Remove the skin from the sausages and shallow fry the sausage meat, breaking
   it up into small pieces. Once cooked, add the fennel, thyme and chilli flakes
   and continue to fry for 2-3 minutes. Add the tinned cherry tomatoes and bring
   to a simmer.
2. In a separate pan, cook the spaghetti as per the instructions. Once ready,
   transfer the spaghetti directly to the sausage pan, allowing some of the
   pasta water to come with it to help make the sauce.
3. Optionally; serve with grated parmesan cheese.
