oven cooked jacket potatoes

the perfect jacket potato with crispy skin, perfect for chilli topped skins.

info

  • 120 minutes
  • 2 servings

ingredients

  • 4 large jacket potatoes
  • olive oil
  • salt and pepper

steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C fan.
  2. Wash, dry and prick the potatoes.
  3. Cover in a generous amount of olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Place them in the oven for 2 hours for crispy jacket potatoes.
