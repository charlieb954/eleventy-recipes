sausage and fennel tomato spaghetti

sausage and fennel in a chopped tomato sauce served with spaghetti.

info

  • About 30 minutes
  • 3-4 servings

ingredients

  • 150g dried spaghetti
  • 8 sausages
  • 400g chopped tomatoes
  • 400g tinned cherry tomatoes
  • 1 tsp fennel seeds
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1 tsp chilli flakes
  • salt and pepper
